About Me

I have always been involved in technology and solving problems to help improve experiences for others. For 4 years, I applied my skillset to the Building Automation Industry and after working alongside the development team my interest peaked and I decided that I wanted to dive into web development. I started my journey with the Covalence Full Stack Bootcamp and then became an Instructor. At Covalence I coached students as they learned about the command line, Git, CS fundamentals, DOM manipulation, OOP concepts, APIs, Server-Side JS, and SQL databases.

I organized a monthly Coding Group for 6 months, where I mentored Junior Developers.

I am currently a Teacher Assistant at Trilogy Education Services and Work full time as an Application Developer Consultant for Stantec where I design, create and develop business solutions.

I love learning new tech, coaching, and working on projects that challenge me.
